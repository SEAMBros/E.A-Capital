Investment Strategy

Our philosophy is simple: bet big on the boldest ideas. We identify the innovators capable of reshaping industries and give them the resources to do so. Our four pillars:

  • Infrastructure: Building smart cities and sustainable energy grids.
  • Space & Frontier Tech: Investing in humanity’s next home.
